In this project, I created a cohesive illustration series that unfolds as a visual narrative, depicting a girl’s journey as she confronts her subconscious fears within her dreamscape. Each illustration functions as a chapter in the story, revealing different emotional states, symbols, and psychological tensions as the boundary between dream and reality becomes increasingly fluid.

Weaving Dreams

Others / Illustration

Duration Oct 2021 - Oct 2022
Role Illustrator
Tools Watercolour, Gouache, Procreate

My question throughout the investigation is "How can I combine Freud's theory of subconsciousness and dreams through my artworks?" Therefore, I decided to create a narrative series of work to show a girl's confrontation with her inner fear in her dreams.

Throughout my body of work, I used strings as a symbol of dream to bring the abstract images of dreams into reality. The strings appear throughout the works to show a blend of fantasies into reality.
