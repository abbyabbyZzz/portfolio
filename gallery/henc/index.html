Crafting Clay, Creating Community.

Henc is more than just a ceramics shop—it is a creative hub where craftsmanship, artistry, and community come together. We offer a carefully curated selection of high-quality, handcrafted ceramic products while also providing a welcoming space for you to explore the art of ceramics. Whether you’re a beginner looking to experience pottery-making for the first time or a seasoned artist refining your skills, Henc is a place where creativity flourishes.

Our mission is to offer high-quality, handcrafted ceramics that inspire creativity and connection. We wish to support local artisans and create a welcoming space for everyone to appreciate and make their own ceramic art.
Henc envisions a creative and community-oriented space where people can explore traditional ceramic-making techniques, connect with others, and engage in hands-on artistic expression.

Logos

Our primary logo is the most distinctive element that represents Henc. With its soft, flowing silhouette, the logotype embodies the tenderness and warmth of ceramic clay, reflecting the handcrafted essence of our brand. The flower symbolizes creativity and artistic expression, representing the unique and personal touch that each of our customers brings to their ceramic-making experience.

If the primary logo cannot be used due to space constraints, the vertical logo serves as an alternative option. This secondary logo maintains Henc’s identity while ensuring flexibility in different applications.

Henc

Graphic Design

Duration Jan - Feb 2025
Role Visual designer
Tools Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop

Henc is a ceramic brand developed to demonstrate creativity, craftsmanship, and community. Rooted in the values of community and making, Henc aims to be a warm and motivating brand that encourages self-expression as well as connection through creation.

As the visual designer, I designed a cohesive identity package for Henc, a ceramic company, including a distinctive logo and color palette. Then, I developed a detailed guideline outlining best practices for logo usage, font hierarchy, and brand voice, ensuring consistent application across all materials.
